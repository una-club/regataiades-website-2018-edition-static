Partnership

Strong values

The Régataïades is a historical university rowing regatta. Since almost 40 years it promotes the values of cohesion, of team spirit, of success to the youth and college students.

Visibility

The partnership with Regataiades is an opportunity to improve regional visibility as well as make you known amongst students in european colleges and universities.

Social footpring

The collaboration with the Régataïades is not only an adherence to the regatta's values but also a chance to contribute to the development of the youth and the sport.

Volonteers

Information and hosting

The boathouse opens Friday night and will remain open until Sunday. Hosts will welcome teams and will provide inforormation to all the participants.

On water security

On water security is crucial and a dedicated team will be working Saturday and Sunday. Designated drivers will assist referees during the races as well as regulate traffic (boat driver licence is reauired). Dockmasters will take in charge the docks traffic.

Transport and logistics

To make the regata run smoothly, shuttles will provide rides from the boathouse to the race course area. Drivers are needed for the shuttles for during the regatta Saturday and Sunday.

Food court

During the whole weekend a food court will be open prividing traditional local meals such as crepes and galettes. You have a chance to contribute by cooking and selling the meals to the participants and spectators.
